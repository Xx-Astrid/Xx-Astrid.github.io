How to make a 10min MUD Cake!!

Ingredients

  • 2 tea-spoon of gluten free self raising flour
  • 2 tea-spoon brown sugar
  • 2 tea-spoon Dutcha cocoa powder
  • 1/4 gluten free baking powder
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tea-spoon of milk
  • 1 tea-spoon of oil
  • 2 tea-spoon of nutella
  • double cream or ice-cream to serve
  • gluten free chocolate sauce

Step 1

Combine the flour,sugar,cocoa powder and baking powder in a bowl

Step 2

Make a well in the centre and add the egg, milk and oil. Use electric beaters to beat until wel combined.

Step 3

Spoon into a 435ml (1 3/4 cup) mug. Spoon the Nutella into the centre. Microwave on High/1000watts/100% for 70 seconds or until risen. Top with double cream or ice-cream and drizzle with chocolate sauce.

Recipe Notes

For a non gluten-free version, use regular self-raising flour and reduce the cooking time to 60 seconds.
